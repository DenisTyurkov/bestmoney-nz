Understanding Car Insurance in NZ

Comprehensive Insurance

The most complete coverage available. Protects your car and others from accidents, theft, fire, and weather damage. Recommended for newer or more valuable vehicles.

Third Party, Fire & Theft

Covers damage you cause to other people's property, plus protection if your car is stolen or damaged by fire. A middle-ground option for mid-value vehicles.

Third Party Only

The minimum recommended coverage. Only pays for damage you cause to other vehicles and property. Best for older cars with low value.

Tips to Lower Your Premium

  • Choose a higher excess (if you can afford it at claim time)
  • Install approved security devices
  • Bundle with other insurance (home, contents)
  • Maintain a clean driving record
  • Compare quotes from multiple providers

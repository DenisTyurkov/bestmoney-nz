What to Consider When Choosing a Personal Loan

Interest Rate

Personal loan rates in NZ range from about 8.99% to 29.99%. Your rate depends on your credit score, income, and the lender. Always compare the total cost, not just the monthly payment.

Loan Term

Terms typically range from 1-7 years. Shorter terms mean higher monthly payments but less interest overall. Longer terms lower your payments but cost more in interest.

Fees

Watch out for establishment fees, monthly service fees, and early repayment fees. Some lenders advertise low rates but charge high fees. Look at the total cost of the loan.

Flexibility

Can you make extra repayments without penalty? What happens if you need to skip a payment? Choose a loan that fits your financial situation.

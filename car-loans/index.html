Car Loan Tips for Kiwis

Get Pre-Approved First

Before you start car shopping, get pre-approved for a loan. This tells you your budget and gives you negotiating power at the dealer. Most banks offer quick pre-approval online.

Secured vs Unsecured

Secured loans use the car as collateral and offer lower rates. Unsecured loans are more flexible but cost more. For most buyers, secured is the better choice.

Watch the Total Cost

A longer loan term means lower payments but more interest paid overall. Use our comparison to see the true cost of each option, not just the monthly payment.

Consider Your Options

Don't just accept dealer finance - compare it with bank loans first. Dealers may offer promotional rates on new cars, but bank loans often beat standard dealer rates.
